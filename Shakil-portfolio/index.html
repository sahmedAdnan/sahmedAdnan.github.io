UPCOMING RESEARCH

Title: Video Summarization in Textual Formation: A Parallelism Survey of Methods, Frameworks, Results and Future Agenda

The survey mostly focuses on the most recent visual captioning researches from 2016 to 2022. However, our overview shows that regardless of the quick-moving turns of events, visual captioning research work is as yet at its outset because of the difficulties of analyzing visual descriptions as well as accuracy and faults of the visual structures and the obtained language. Lastly, we finish up our review by posting promising future directions.
